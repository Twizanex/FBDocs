Platform Policies and Promotions Checklist

Supplemental Materials › Platform Policies and Promotions Checklist

Date of Last Revision: August 10, 2011

The Platform Policy and Promotions Guidelines Checklist is a guide to reviewing your app’s features and functionality at the concept stage of development and just prior to launch. While the checklist is not an exhaustive list of the Facebook Platform Policies and Promotions Guidelines, it provides a quick “sanity check” to help ensure your app does not contain any easily-avoidable policy violations.
