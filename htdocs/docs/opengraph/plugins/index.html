Social Plugins

Core Concepts › Open Graph › Social Plugins

Social plugins let you see what your friends have liked, commented on or shared on sites across the web.

Recommendations Bar

Recommendations Bar allows users to share what they are reading with their friends, like content and recieve personalized recommendations.

Recommendations Box

Recommendations Box shows personalized recommendations to your users for pages on your site. The plugin can display personalized recommendations whether or not the user has logged into your site.

Facepile

Facepile plugin displays the Facebook profile pictures of users who have connected with your page via a built-in or custom actions, or can also be configured to display users that have signed up for your site.
Activity Feed

Activity Feed plugin displays the most interesting recent activity taking place on your site. The plugin can display personalized content whether or not the user has logged into your site.
