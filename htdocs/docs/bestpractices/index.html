Best Practices

Getting Started › Best Practices

This section contains best practices, guidelines, and design patterns. The purpose of these documents is to help product managers and developers understand the most effective ways to integrate with the Facebook Platform. Our goal is to complement our developer documentation by answering common questions and highlighting important aspects of the platform. These documents are both conceptual (how to think about building social experiences) and tactical (how to implement).

We will be continuously adding and updating these documents and cover additions in the weekly Platform Updates on the Developer Blog.
